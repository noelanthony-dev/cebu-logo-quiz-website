About the Game

Why We Created Cebu Logo Quiz

We wanted to celebrate Cebu's rich local business culture and help both locals and tourists connect with the brands that make our island special. This game is our love letter to Cebu's entrepreneurial spirit.

What Makes It Unique

  • Cebu-only content – Exclusively featuring local Cebuano brands
  • Trivia system – Learn interesting facts about each brand
  • Progress badges – Earn titles like "Cebu Expert" as you advance
